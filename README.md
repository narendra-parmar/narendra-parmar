### 🎵 1. Chinook Business Intelligence — SQL + Power BI
> **Tools:** MySQL · SQL · Power BI · DAX
> 🔗 [View Repository](https://github.com/narendra-parmar/chinook-business-intelligence)

**About the Dataset:** The Chinook database simulates a digital music store (like iTunes), containing real-world relational data across customers, invoices, tracks, albums, artists, genres, employees, and playlists.

**What I Did:**
- Wrote complex **SQL queries** using JOINs, CTEs, subqueries, and window functions to extract business-critical insights
- Analyzed **customer purchasing behaviour** across countries and time periods
- Identified **top-selling artists, albums, and genres** to uncover revenue drivers
- Evaluated **employee sales performance** and regional contribution metrics
- Built an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to visualize KPIs, trends, and drill-down reports

**📊 Key Insights Uncovered:**
- 🌍 USA contributed the highest revenue among all countries
- 🎶 Rock was the dominant genre, accounting for the largest share of track sales
- 📅 Identified peak sales months to support promotional planning
- 👤 Top 10% of customers drove a disproportionate share of total revenue
- 🧑‍💼 Sales performance varied significantly across employees — enabling targeted coaching

**📌 Skills Demonstrated:** SQL Aggregations · Window Functions · CTEs · Power BI Dashboarding · Business Reporting

### 🍽️ 2. Zomato Global Restaurant Analysis — Excel
> **Tools:** Microsoft Excel · Pivot Tables · Power Query · Charts & Slicers
> 🔗 [View Repository](https://github.com/narendra-parmar/Zomato-Global-Restaurant-Analysis)

**About the Dataset:** A global dataset of 9,500+ restaurants across 15 countries and 140+ cities from Zomato, covering cuisine types, ratings, cost, delivery options, and geographic data.

**What I Did:**
- Cleaned and transformed raw restaurant data using **Power Query** — handled nulls, duplicates, and inconsistent formatting
- Built **dynamic Pivot Tables** to summarize restaurant counts, average ratings, and pricing by country and city
- Designed an **interactive Excel Dashboard** with slicers, timelines, and conditional formatting
- Analyzed **cuisine popularity**, cost brackets, and delivery availability across regions
- Used **advanced Excel formulas** (VLOOKUP, IF, COUNTIF, AVERAGEIFS) for custom KPI calculations

**📊 Key Insights Uncovered:**
- 🌏 India had the highest number of listed restaurants, followed by USA and UK
- ⭐ Restaurants offering **online delivery** had a notably higher average rating
- 💰 North Indian and Chinese cuisines dominated the mid-price segment globally
- 🏙️ New Delhi had the highest restaurant density among all cities in the dataset
- 📊 Interactive slicers allowed filtering by country, cuisine, and rating band for quick business decisions

**📌 Skills Demonstrated:** Excel Dashboarding · Power Query · Pivot Tables · Data Cleaning · Advanced Formulas · Data Storytelling
